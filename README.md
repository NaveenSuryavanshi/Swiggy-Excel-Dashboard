Food Delivery Sales & Performance Dashboard (Power BI)
📌 Project Overview
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, restaurant trends, and regional insights for a food delivery platform.
The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business intelligence to support strategic decision-making.


🎯 Business Objective
The goal of this project is to:
Track revenue and order performance
Analyze customer engagement through ratings and rating count
Identify high-performing cities and states
Understand monthly and daily sales trends
Compare Veg vs Non-Veg food sales distribution
Monitor Average Order Value (AOV) and overall business health
This dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate operational performance and identify growth opportunities.


📊 Key KPIs
Total Sales: ₹53.01M
Total Orders: 197.43K
Average Rating: 4.34
Total Rating Count: 5.59M
Average Order Value (AOV): ₹268.51

📈 Dashboard Features

Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
Daily Sales Performance Breakdown
State-wise Sales Distribution (Map Visualization)
Top 5 Cities by Sales
Sales by Food Type (Veg vs Non-Veg)
Quarterly Sales, Rating & Orders Comparison
Interactive Filters (Month, Restaurant, Category)
