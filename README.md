**Food Delivery Sales & Performance Dashboard (Power BI)**
_**📌 Project Overview**_
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, restaurant trends, and regional insights for a food delivery platform.
The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business intelligence to support strategic decision-making.


_**🎯 Business Objective**_
1. The goal of this project is to:
2. Track revenue and order performance
3. Analyze customer engagement through ratings and rating count
4. Identify high-performing cities and states
5. Understand monthly and daily sales trends
6. Compare Veg vs Non-Veg food sales distribution
7. Monitor Average Order Value (AOV) and overall business health
8. This dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate operational performance and identify growth opportunities.


_**📊 Key KPIs**_
1. Total Sales: ₹53.01M
2. Total Orders: 197.43K
3. Average Rating: 4.34
4. Total Rating Count: 5.59M
5. Average Order Value (AOV): ₹268.51

_**📈 Dashboard Features**_
1. Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
2. Daily Sales Performance Breakdown
3. State-wise Sales Distribution (Map Visualization)
4. Top 5 Cities by Sales
5. Sales by Food Type (Veg vs Non-Veg)
6. Quarterly Sales, Rating & Orders Comparison
7. Interactive Filters (Month, Restaurant, Category)
